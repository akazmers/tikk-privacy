TIKK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

TIKK ("we", "our", "the app") is a focus timer application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

TIKK stores your settings, statistics, and purchase status locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not transmit this data to any server.

If you purchase TIKK Pro, our payment processors (Stripe, Apple, or Google) handle your payment information. We do not store credit card numbers or billing details.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense / AdMob: Displays ads to free users. Google may use cookies and device identifiers for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Processes web payments. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app purchases on mobile. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple / Google: Process in-app purchases on their respective platforms.

Cookies & Tracking

The web version uses localStorage (not cookies) for app functionality. Third-party ad providers may use cookies for ad targeting. You can control this via your browser or device settings.

Children's Privacy

TIKK is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
